How to Practice Green therapy?

    Ways to Incorporate More Nature into your Daily Life:

  • Spend two hours or more each week outdoors in natural settings, in either short or long blocks of time
  • Bring plants, flowers and other reminders of nature into work and home settings.
  • Garden outdoors
  • Keep a kitchen herb garden indoors
  • Use nature scenes as screensavers
